Tradiotional Volleyball Tournament
TIK - TIF - Sonera
Loppi 14.-15.9.2002
The tournament took place at Elmola -sportshouse in Loppi. Weather was chilly and windy but mostly sunny.

Co-operation is power! Here we have one borrowed TIK player under Soneras "umbrella".

Hard concentration and some good lukc was once again the winning recipe.

Spectators seem to enjoy the atmosphere of excitement.

After matches it was time to relax, first with refreshments and...
...then by bathing in sauna. Laakasalo was the holiday village where the teams were quartering.

Hot sauna, cold water and hard wind. What more a real man could dream of?
Well, perhaps cool drinks and a few other things...

The future looks good. We have a new "Finnkampare" generation growing up.

After good dinner, it was time to analyze the ups and downs in the matches of the day, as well as in the "matches" of our lives - in several groups and...

...in several languages.
And after this point of the evening, there was no more pictures in my memory....(cameras memory :)

 

On behalf of me and my "family", I want to thank you all for - once again - memorable weekend that we spent together. I wish, that despite of all the changes around us, we still could continue this tradiotion. In this insecure world we do need some stabel things, something that we can wait for to come as sure as the sun is rising in the morning.
